# OKF v0.1 (condensed capture)

OKF represents structured knowledge as a directory of markdown files
with YAML frontmatter, prioritizing human readability, agent
parsability, version control, and portability.

## Bundle architecture
- Bundles are directory trees; distribute via git (recommended),
  tarball, zip, or subdirectory of a larger repo.
- Reserved filenames (§3.1): `index.md` (directory listing) and
  `log.md` (update history). All other `.md` files are concepts.

## Concept documents
- Frontmatter: required `type` (free-form string, no registry;
  consumers tolerate unknown types). Recommended: `title`,
  `description`, `resource`, `tags`, `timestamp`. Producers may add
  arbitrary extension keys; consumers must preserve unknown keys.
- Body: no required sections. Conventional headings: `# Schema`,
  `# Examples`, `# Citations`. Prefer structural markdown over prose.

## Cross-linking (§5)
- Bundle-relative links `[t](/path/x.md)` recommended (stable under
  moves); relative links allowed. Links are untyped directed edges;
  relationship meaning lives in surrounding prose. Broken links are
  tolerated — they may denote not-yet-written knowledge.

## Reserved files
- `index.md` (§6): no frontmatter (except bundle root, which may carry
  `okf_version`); sections of `* [Title](url) - description` entries;
  may be auto-generated.
- `log.md` (§7): `## YYYY-MM-DD` headings, newest first; entries
  conventionally start with a bold verb (`**Update**`).

## Citations (§8)
Numbered references under `# Citations`: `[1] [Title](url)`. May point
at external URLs, bundle paths, or mirrored reference subdirectories.

## Conformance (§9)
Conformant iff: every non-reserved `.md` has parseable frontmatter with
non-empty `type`, and reserved files follow their structures when
present. Consumers must NOT reject bundles for missing optional fields,
unknown types/keys, broken links, or absent indexes.

## Versioning (§11)
Current: v0.1. Minor bumps = backward-compatible additions; major =
breaking. Bundles may declare `okf_version: "0.1"` in bundle-root
index.md frontmatter.

## Philosophy
Minimal by design: no fixed taxonomies, no storage/serving
prescriptions, references domain schemas (Avro/Protobuf/OpenAPI) rather
than replacing them.
